Our Love Story

  • First We Meet

    Fall 2006

    Freshman year of high school, history class. Kurt thought Calyn's name was Gallon...we're off to a good start.

  • First Date

    Fall 2009

    After Kurt learned Calyn's name, they grew to eventually become best friends. Calyn was adamant that their relationship was platonic, but things began to change once she saw how cute Kurt was dressed for the homecoming dance!

  • In A Relationship

    November 28, 2009

    After a lengthy period of inner turmoil, Calyn finally conceded.

    "I guess we are dating." -Calyn

    Again, off to a great start!

  • The Big Move

    Summer 2015

    After a five year long distance relationship, during which Calyn and Kurt worked on their college degrees, Kurt joined Calyn in Florida! Together at last!!

    Freed from the perils of winter, Kurt and Calyn enjoy frequent trips to the beach and wearing shorts year round.

  • We're Engaged!

    November 25, 2017

    On the most magical of days, Kurt and Calyn ventured to Hogwarts (also known as Orlando, FL). While taking pictures in front of the castle, Kurt surprised Calyn with the proposal. Unlike our previous relationship status change, this was an immediate YES!!
